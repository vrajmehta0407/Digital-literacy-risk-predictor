# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: 2024**

## 1. Data Collection
We do not collect or upload your personal data. 
*   **SMS Content**: Analyzed locally on-device. Discarded immediately after analysis.
*   **Call Data**: Numbers are checked locally.
*   **Guardian Info**: Stored locally in encrypted preferences.

## 2. Data Sharing
We do not share data with third parties.
*   **Exception**: If you designate a "Guardian", the app will send an SMS to them on your behalf only when a high-risk threat is detected or you press the Panic Button.

## 3. Offline Functionality
The app functions fully offline. No internet connection is required for the core scam detection to work.

## 4. Contact
For support, please contact your local Digital Literacy helpline.
